Common questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Here are the things we hear most often. Don't see yours? Give us a call — we love a good HVAC question.

Ask us anything
How fast can you come out for an emergency?

For most of Marion County, we can be at your door within 1–2 hours during business hours, and same-day for emergency calls 24/7. Hottest weeks of the year may be longer — call us first thing.

Do you give free estimates?

Yes — every install or replacement quote is 100% free, no obligation. We come on-site, measure, listen to what you need, and walk you through real options at every budget level.

How long does a new system install take?

Most residential AC and heat pump replacements take a single day. Larger jobs, ductwork changes, or commercial installs can take 2–4 days. We always give you a clear timeline before we start.

What brands do you install?

We install premium brands — Trane, Daikin, Carrier, Rheem and Ruud — and value brands like Goodman and RunTru. We're not tied to any single manufacturer, which means we recommend what's actually best for your home.

Are you licensed and insured?

Fully licensed (FL CAC1823749) and insured. We rank in the top 27% of all licensed contractors in Florida according to BuildZoom.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We work with several financing partners offering 0% promotional periods and low monthly payment plans on full system replacements. Ask us during your estimate.

What's covered under your warranty?

New installations come with manufacturer warranties up to 12 years on parts (varies by brand). Our workmanship is also backed — if we install it, we stand behind it.
